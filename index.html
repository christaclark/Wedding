OUR BIG DAY

WEDDING DETAILS

CEREMONY

The ceremony will be held outside, on the lawn of the Barr Mansion. We recommend layering, so please bring something warm in case it gets chilly.

RECEPTION

After the ceremony we will move into the beautiful, glass barn for dinner, drinks, and dance! We have the space boked until late so be ready to have some fun!

ACCOMODATION

We have 15 rooms booked at the La Quinta Inn North, 7622 IH 35 N, Austin, TX 78752. We can add on rooms if needed, however please book your room by February 7th. You can request a king bed or two queens for $104 a night.

WEATHER

Historically this have been a warm weekend! The past few years it has averaged in the high 70's. However be prepared for the weather to range from 40 to 80 degrees, as you know weather in Austin, TX.
